Projects

Below are links to projects I've worked on. All were built using WordPress; some required customization and/or management of a Multisite installation.


My academic site

My academic website, where I maintain a blog and where you'll find links to a number of course websites. The main site and most of the course sites run on a Wordpress Multisite installation.

Saint Mary's Commons

At Saint Mary's College, the classes of 2018, 2019, and 2020 were part of an ePortfolio pilot project. They created their portfolio sites using a WordPress Multisite installation that I set up and continue to maintain. (Students from the class of 2021 onwards have been creating their portfolios in Blackboard.)

Women of Holy Cross 175th

This was a website for the 175th anniversary of the Women of Holy Cross (now the Marianites of Holy Cross, Sisters of Holy Cross, and Sisters of the Holy Cross). Originally it had its own domain, but when the registration lapsed I moved it to my WordPress Multisite installation.
