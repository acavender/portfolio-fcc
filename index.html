DEVELOPER. PROFESSOR. WRITER.

I'm a web developer and an Associate Professor at Saint Mary's College, where I teach in the Political Science department. In addition to teaching, I set up and continue to maintain the Saint Mary's Commons, a multisite WordPress network that our sophomores, juniors, and seniors use for their ePortfolios. I'm also active with the college's Teaching, Learning, and Technology Roundtable. I blog at ProfHacker.


PORTFOLIO

Projects I've worked on:

Women of Holy Cross


I designed and built (others contributed the content) the Women of Holy Cross site for the celebration of the 175th anniversary of the founding of the women's branches of the Holy Cross family. (There are also brothers and priests of Holy Cross.) Originally, the site had its own domain, but the registration lapsed so I moved the site to its current location on my own domain.

Saint Mary's Commons


I built and maintain (with help from the Saint Mary's IT department) the Saint Mary's Commons. The Commons is a multisite WordPress installation that launched in August 2014 as a pilot project. Students in the Saint Mary's classes of 2018, 2019, and 2020 maintain their ePortfolios in the Commons.

My academic site

My professional academic site is the primary site in a WordPress multisite network. Most of my course sites are also part of the network, though the site for this semester's course (Spring 2018) is on GitHub.
